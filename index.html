About us

Our Business, Mission, Goals and aims

Established in 2023, Metro Coast Trading Est. was founded by a team of seasoned professionals dedicated to serving the industrial needs of Saudi Arabia. We specialize in distributing high-quality electrical, mechanical, instrumentation, and steel products designed to meet the ever-evolving requirements of modern industrial projects. We aim to provide more than just materials—we deliver tailored solutions that address our clients' unique challenges, prioritizing quality and reliability.

At Metro Coast, strong client relationships are the cornerstone of our success. Driven by a commitment to innovation, we continuously enhance our products and services to match industry advancements and ensure exceptional customer satisfaction. Whether you require specialized equipment or comprehensive project support, we are here to be your trusted partner in industrial excellence.

Our Vision

To be a leader in industrial supply, recognized for exceptional quality, innovation, and unwavering integrity. We aim to support the growth and success of our clients by providing reliable, high-performing solutions. At Metro Coast, we envision a future where our commitment to excellence shapes industry standards.

Our Mission

To deliver top-quality industrial products and solutions that empower our clients to achieve their goals. We are dedicated to continuous improvement, ensuring that our products and services evolve with the needs of the industries we serve. By fostering strong partnerships, we aim to create lasting value for our clients and communities.
